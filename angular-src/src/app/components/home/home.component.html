Undergraduate Students

Undergraduate Students can register for the end semester examinations through this portal. Just login through you username and password and simply register for relevant courses.

Postgraduate Students

Postgraduate Students who are doing Masters at UCSC can register for the end semester examinations through this portal. Just Register and login through you username and password and simply register for relevant courses.

Repeat Students

Repeat Students can apply for repeat examinations through this portal. Simply follow instructions for Undergraduate Students or Postgraduate Students
